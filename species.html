About Species

SPECIES LORE/INFO:

Zippleons, often affectionately referred to as "zipps," are a captivating species that combines the characteristics of fluffy snakes and chameleons. They possess the remarkable ability to shift between anthropomorphic and feral forms, and their humanoid shape can be accessed by consuming a special potion known as the human form potion

Zippleons are celebrated for their lively and playful nature, making them a truly unique addition to the world of fantastical creatures.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION:

1. Head Structure:

  • Semi-rectangular, snake-like shape.
  • basic, almond, eepy shaped eyes.
  • Heat pits on the sides of their face to help regulate temperature.

2. Horns:

  • Inward-twisting horns; nub, small, or medium in size

3. Charms:

  • Found within their manes beneath their horns. Can be on one or both sides and must be based on the theme of the zippleon

4. Bodies:

  • Range of shapes, from slender to plump.

5. Tails:

  • Curly and fluffy with a zipper pouch for storing items.

6. Diet:

  • Diverse preferences including meat, fruit, berries, insects, and sweets.

7. Mutations:

  • Allow for unique features and variations.

Notably, Zippleons are a CLOSED species created by SinisterGutzand individuals can only create their own Zippleons if they possess a MYO (Make Your Own) egg.

REQUIRED TRAITS:

Mandatory traits for all Zippleons unless mutations are applied

Head Structure:

  • Rectangular, snake-like head with optional slight nose ridge (mutations allowed for modifications)

Eye Color and Shape:

  • Eyes are super dark or black; mutations required for other colors
  • Eye shapes: basic almond or eepy

Tongue:

  • Forked or pointy tongue

Tail:

  • Curly and fluffy tail
  • Tail can be curled up or down

Mane:

  • Fluffy mane covering head and neck
  • Extremely large manes require mutations for justification

Charms:

  • Charm under horns on side(s) of mane

Paw Structure:

  • Big hand paws, smaller feet paws
  • No claws unless mutated

Facial Features:

  • Snake (python) heat pits for cooling (no nostrils)
  • Earless

Tail Features:

  • Tail zipper of any shape and size

Horns:

  • Inward twisting horns unless mutated

Digits:

  • 3 to 4 toes and fingers; mutations allowed for modifications

Teeth:

  • No teeth unless mutated

Paw Pads:

  • Soft "peach" shaped paw pads on hands and feet
  • Modifications through mutations possible

Please note: These traits are standard for Zippleons. Deviations or additional features require mutations for justification and incorporation into an individual Zippleon's design.
